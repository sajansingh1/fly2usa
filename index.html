Interview Preparation

Master your visa interview with expert guidance and practice

Common Interview Questions

Q1

Why do you want to study in the USA?

Focus on your academic and career goals. Explain how the specific university or program aligns with your objectives.
Q2

What are your plans after graduation?

Show clear intent to return home. Discuss your career plans and how US education will help you succeed in your home country.
Q3

How will you finance your education?

Have specific documentation ready. Clearly explain your financial support sources.
Q4
What is your intended major and why?
Show genuine interest and knowledge about your field. Connect it to your career goals..
